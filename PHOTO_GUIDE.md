1. **Dress Code:**
   - **Business Casual** or **Smart Casual**

4. **Lighting:**
   - **Natural, even lighting**
   - Avoid harsh shadows
   - Professional studio lighting is ideal

5. **Photo Quality:**
   - **High resolution** (at least 800x800px)
   - Sharp, clear image
   - Good color balance
   - Professional photography recommended
